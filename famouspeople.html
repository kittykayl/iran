Leila Forouhar is an Iranian singer and actor. She was born on February 23rd, 1959. She has been acting since she was three years old. Her mom's name is Farangis and her dad's name is Jahangir. She got married to Essi Nabi in 2004.

Mohammad Reza Golzar is a famous Iranian actor. He was born on March 21st, 1977. His mom's name is Shahnaz and his dad's name is Parviz. He also has a brother named Alireza and a sister named Atousa.
