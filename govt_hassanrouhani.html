Hassan Rouhani

Hassan Rouhani is the President of Iran. He was born on November 12th, 1948 in Sorkheh, Iran. He married Sahebeh Rouhani in 1968. He has been a lawyer, academic, diplomat, and an Islamic cleric. He became president on August 3rd, 2013. He was arrested many times in the 1960s and 1970s as a follower of Ayatollah Khomeini.
